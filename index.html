Three crews. Two meals. Eternal bragging rights.

Groom decides

Each Cocina Crew owns breakfast and lunch for their assigned day. Menus are completely up to the crew. The goal is simple: keep everyone fed, make it memorable, and leave the kitchen ready for the next team.

Cocina Rules

  • Plan your own breakfast and lunch menu.
  • Use the groceries available at the Casa.
  • Meals should work around the weekend schedule.
  • Clean the kitchen before handing it to the next crew.
  • Cesar crowns the Cocina Champions.
