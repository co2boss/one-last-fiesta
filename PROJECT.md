# One Last Fiesta Before the Siesta

**Codename:** Campfire

**Status:** RC1 – Sunrise

---

# Vision

One Last Fiesta Before the Siesta is more than a bachelor party itinerary.

It is a premium interactive travel experience designed to bring friends together before, during, and after an unforgettable weekend.

Every traveler becomes part of the story.

The app should feel less like software and more like a handcrafted experience.

---

# Mission Statement

> Every legend has a role.
>
> Every fiesta has a story.

---

# Core Philosophy

This application exists to create memories.

Every feature should accomplish at least one of the following:

- Bring the crew together
- Build excitement
- Encourage interaction
- Preserve memories
- Make someone smile

If a feature does not accomplish one of these goals, it should not be added.

---

# Design Principles

## Premium First

The application should feel like a luxury travel experience.

Inspired by:

- Apple
- Airbnb
- Four Seasons
- Luxury Tequila Brands

---

## Story Before Screens

The app is not a collection of pages.

The app tells a story.

Each interaction moves the traveler through a new chapter.

---

## Simplicity Wins

Every screen should feel effortless.

No unnecessary buttons.

No clutter.

---

## Campfire Principle

Every feature should strengthen the connection between the crew.

---

## The Groom Comes First

Everything should support the experience of
**El Rey de la Fiesta**.
